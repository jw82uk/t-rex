Unmatched Bone-Crushing Bite Force

While earlier predators sliced flesh, T. rex evolved to crush entirely through bone, boasting an estimated bite force of up to 12,800 pounds per square inch. That's a lot of force, enough force to crush a car!

Think of it this way. It's the equivalent of 4 of our biggest, strongest living crocodiles, or over 100 angry Chihuahuas!
